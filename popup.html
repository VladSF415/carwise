Dealer Tactics — Know Before You Go
Private Party & Craigslist Guard
HIGH RISK Title Washing ⌄
Sellers move flood/salvage cars across state lines to obtain clean titles. ~450,000 cars laundered annually. Always run a VIN check and verify title status with your state DMV.
HIGH RISK Odometer Fraud ⌄
Check for worn pedals and seats on "low mileage" cars. Service records showing higher odometer readings are a dead giveaway. Run Carfax or AutoCheck.
CAUTION Cashier's Check Fraud ⌄
Fake cashier's checks are common in private sales. Wait for check to fully clear at your bank (2–5 business days) before handing over keys.
CAUTION Outstanding Liens ⌄
Seller may still owe money on the car. A lienholder shown on title means you could inherit the debt. Verify lien is satisfied before purchase.
